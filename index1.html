Why Helion Exists

Improving decision quality at inflection points

Many development delays are not caused by poor science, but by early decisions that were never fully stress-tested.

Helion exists to improve decision quality at development inflection points — where small compromises can later create disproportionate regulatory and CMC risk. We operate at the interface of science, safety, formulation, and strategy.

Helion is led by Senthil Murugappan, a senior development and CMC professional with deep experience across vaccines, biologics, mRNA, and novel delivery platforms. His work focuses on early technical judgement, risk identification, and decision clarity at points where development pathways become difficult to change.

CMC Strategy

Why CMC Liabilities Are Invisible Until They Are Not

The most damaging CMC issues rarely announce themselves at preclinical stage. They accumulate quietly in decisions that seem rational in isolation but create compound risk over time.

Formulation

Formulation Is a Decision, Not a Process

Too many early-stage programs treat formulation as something that gets resolved by the CRO during scale-up. By then, the decisions have already been made — just not by you.

Platform Risk

Novel Platforms and the Credibility Gap

Delivery platform selection often happens under commercial pressure, with limited independent technical scrutiny. The gap between what platforms claim and what they can demonstrably deliver remains significant.
